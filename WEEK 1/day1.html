Mini Fudgy Brownie Bites

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making mini fudgy brownie bites! These treats are perfect for satisfying your chocolate cravings without overindulging. Follow this simple recipe to create delicious, crispy-on-the-outside, gooey-on-the-inside mini brownie bites that everypne will love.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsaltered butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350`F (175`C) and grease a mini muffin tin or line with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until well combined.
  4. Sift in the cocoa powder, flour, and salt, and stir until just combined. Fold in the mini chocolate chips if desired.
  5. Spoon the batter into the mini muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 10-20 minutes, then let cool before enjoying.
